# Prompt Engineering Framework

## Objective

Generate an SEO-optimized blog article for a local café business.

---

## Prompt

Act as an experienced SEO content writer.

Generate a professional blog for Brew Haven Café.

Include:

- SEO Title
- Meta Description
- H1 Heading
- H2 Headings
- H3 Headings
- Introduction
- Body Content
- FAQ Section
- Conclusion
- Call-to-Action

Requirements:

- SEO-friendly
- Human-readable
- Keyword optimized
- Original content
- Professional tone
- Suitable for business websites

Target Audience:

- Students
- Coffee Lovers
- Families
- Professionals

Length:

1000–1200 words

Output the blog in Markdown format.
